# Content & Copy Rules

## Voice

- Gentle, reassuring, calm
- Avoid urgency or pressure
- Avoid productivity guilt framing

## Copy Examples

- Headline: “A softer way to stay productive.”
- Subheadline: “petal helps you focus without pressure, guilt, or burnout.”
- CTA: “Join the waitlist”
- Microcopy: “No pressure — just gentle updates.”

## Do Not Use

- Countdown language
- Aggressive urgency (“Act now”, “Don’t miss out”)
- Competitive productivity comparisons
